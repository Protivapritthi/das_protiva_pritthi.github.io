🧪Research Projects

  • Cyberbiosecurity and Bio-LLM Security
    Ongoing research on improving the security of biological large language models against dual-use misuse and multi-turn jailbreak attacks. This work studies how Bio-LLMs may be manipulated across multi-step conversations and explores defense mechanisms for safer deployment in sensitive biological domains.
  • BioShield: A Context-Aware Firewall for Securing Bio-LLMs
    A research project focused on designing a context-aware defense layer for Bio-LLMs that can detect and block unsafe conversational trajectories, especially in cyberbiosecurity-sensitive settings. The project investigates prompt-level and response-level safeguards for protecting biological AI systems. [Scholar Link]
  • Perceptions and Impacts of Digitally Synthesized Intimate Content in Bangladesh
    An HCI and ethics-focused exploration of AI-generated deepfake imagery and user awareness in conservative societies.
  • Workforce Transitions in Aviation Post-COVID
    A socio-technical study of employment patterns in the aviation industry across pre-, during-, and post-pandemic phases.
